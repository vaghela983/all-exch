BETTING RULES
General Rules
1.1 Market Settlement

All markets are settled based on the official result of the governing body of the respective sport/event.

1.2 Void Bets

If an event is cancelled or postponed for more than 48 hours, all bets will be void unless a result has already been determined.

Cricket Rules
2.1 Match Results

In the event of a tie where no "Super Over" or other tie-breaker is played, Dead Heat rules apply.

2.2 Rain Rule (DLS)

Matches affected by rain will be settled according to the DLS method result if the minimum required overs are completed.

Football Rules
3.1 90 Minute Rule

All bets are settled on the result at the end of 90 minutes of play (including injury time) unless otherwise stated.

Casino Rules
4.1 Malfunction

A malfunction voids all pays and plays. In case of disconnection, the hand will be played out by the system.
